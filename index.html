Hazard Insurance

Our comprehensive coverage includes repatriation of remains, emergency medical airlifting, and high-altitude rescue. Standard policies do not apply in active war zones or deep-sea expeditions without a premium rider.

Extraction Points

Real-time satellite coordinates for our global extraction network. In the event of a comms blackout, proceed to the secondary rally point identified in your pre-trip briefing packet. Look for the infrared strobe signals.

Equipment Rental

Rent professional-grade hardware including satellite phones, Geiger counters, chemical suits, and off-road recovery gear. All equipment is stress-tested in simulated extreme environments before being issued.
