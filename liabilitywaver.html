RELEASE OF LIABILITY

Legal Notice: Operational Hazards

The Participant acknowledges that the "Danger: High" rating implies a statistical probability of non-recovery. In the event of physical expiration, disappearance, or permanent displacement within a designated "Inhospitable Zone," the Participant hereby grants Roamers, Venture & Co. an exclusive, perpetual, and irrevocable license to the Participant’s post-corporeal essence, metaphysical signature, and any lingering consciousness.

I understand that by signing this waiver, I am "selling the rights to my ghost." Should extraction fail and my physical form cease to function, my intangible remains shall become the property of the Company. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • My spiritual presence may be tethered to the coordinates of my demise to serve as a permanent, non-sentient beacon for future expeditions.
  • Use of my residual energy to stabilize local fluctuations in reality or terrain instability.
  • Standing watch over the Company’s remote caches in perpetuity, without the requirement of rest or compensation.

I certify that this transfer is absolute. No religious, philosophical, or familial claims shall supersede this Sovereign Assignment. I recognize that there is no "buy-back" option once the threshold of the expedition is crossed. My soul is henceforth a line item on the Company's balance sheet, categorized under Permanent Assets.

  • Resource Reclamation. The Company reserves the right to harvest any biological or synthetic components from the Participant’s remains (organs, bone marrow, cybernetic implants) if deemed necessary for the survival of the remaining expedition or the ongoing profitability of the venture.

The Participant hereby assigns all rights to any insights, visions, or "Other-Side" data gathered during the transition from life to the assigned Sovereign State.

  • Any vocalizations, "death-rattles," or psychic echoes recorded during the moment of expiration are the intellectual property of the Company and may be used for marketing, navigation, or occult-calibration purposes without royalty.
  • The Company maintains the right to rewrite the Participant’s final words or circumstances of death to better suit the "Roamers, Venture & Co. Legendarium."
